Tweet. Post. Comment. Smile. This era has it's own lingo: a jargon-addled psuedolanguage that sometimes barely resembles recognized speech or written words. Children, teenagers, and adults alike all speak it; as everyone desperately tries to capture fleeting memories with a tweet, photo, or audio clip. Most attempts fail.
