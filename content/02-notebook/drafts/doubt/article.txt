title: Doubt
----
subtitle: Questioning yourself, and how to deal with it effectively.
----
date: 04/02/12
----
text:
Whether art is your job or hobby, you've probably doubted yourself more than once. You questioned whether your creative output is worth the asking price, why anyone should care about your work, or if art is a passion of yours, rather than a futile attempt to present statements of worth to an unkind world. But it's the topic that we, as creators, hardly discuss.
